Instructions:
Objective: The player that connects 4 of the same colored discs in a row (horizontally, diagonally, vertically, or horizontally).
  1. 1. Decide on who goes first and what color each player will have.
  2. 2. Players must alternate turns and only one disc can be dropped in each turn.
  3. 3. On your turn, drop one of your colored dics into any seven slots.
  4. 4. Once a player has four in a row, the game ends.
